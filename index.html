FAQs

The size of team can be 1 to 4 members. Every team must have at least 1 female member and at most 1 male member.

Only female participants are allowed to participate individually as one female member per team is necessary.

The event is scheduled on 22 may 2021-23 may 2021.

BVPIEEE is the student branch of IEEE at BVCOE. IEEE is the world's largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity.

There is no participation fee for the event.

The initial screening of the teams will be based on the LinkedIn profile, GitHub profile and the abstract submitted by the team.

To participate in the event, all team members must be enrolled in an undergraduate program
