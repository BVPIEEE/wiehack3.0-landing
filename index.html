FAQs_

How big can my team be?

The maximum team size allowed is 4 with atmost 1 male member.

Can I come individually?

Yes,only female individual participation is allowed.

When is the event?

WieHack3.0 will be a 36 hour hackathon which will stretch from 22nd May to 23rd May 2021.

What is BVPIEEE?

BVPIEEE is the IEEE student branch for Bharati Vidyapeeth's College of Engineering, New Delhi. Visit our website https://www.bvpieee.com to know more.

How much does it cost?

Everything at WieHack3.0 is free of cost.

What are the criteria of shortlisting the teams?

The registered participants will be shortlisted based on their GitHub and LinkedIn profiles as well as their abstract submissions.

What is the eligibility criteria?

You must be an undergraduate student from any University/College in India.
